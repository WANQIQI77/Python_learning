Industrial internet boosts high-quality development

People attend the Global Industrial Internet Conference in Shenyang, Northeast China's Liaoning province on Nov 7, 2022. [Photo/Xinhua]

Industrial internet boosts high-quality development as is showcased at the 2022 Global Industrial Internet Conference in Shenyang, Northeast China's Liaoning province.

Themed as "Empowering High Quality, Generate New Momentum", the conference, covering 20,000 square meters, highlighted cutting-edge technologies, typical cases and application scenarios of industrial internet.

People can not only play Go with a robot or see a nucleic acid testing robot but they also can have tailor-made clothes at the conference.
